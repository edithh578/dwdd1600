Delivery policy

  1. U.S. orders must be received before 1:00 p.m. in the recipient's time zone to assure same-day delivery. Orders received after that time will be delivered the following day.
  2. We are unable to make deliveries on Sundays. Deliveries requested on this day will be delivered the following business day.
  3. A local delivery fee of $15.00 will be added to each order, for each address. (For deliveries outside our local delivery area, this fee may vary.)
  4. To help assure on-time delivery during the busy holiday season, place your order at least 1 day prior to the following major holidays
